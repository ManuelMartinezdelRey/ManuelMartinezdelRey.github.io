Food Delivery M

Description

App to manage your food delivery. If you own an iPad then you can manage all the orders and clients on a easy way
- Manage your orders.
- Keep the clients of your business.
- The app shows you the route from your business to your client.
- Routes for several orders in map.
- Estimate the order delivery hour.
- Print your order.
